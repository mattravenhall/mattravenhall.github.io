Overview

Genetic variation has been demonstrated to provide individuals with protection against severe malaria, for example through the malaria-protective effect of heterozygous carriage of the sickle cell anaemia allele.[1] My research aims to better understand these variants, and to identify further variants and selection events which may better inform our understanding of malaria.
